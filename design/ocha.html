Ocha Ohana Montblanc

Logo Design

Logo design for a cafe named "Ocha Ohana Montblanc" in Osaka, Japan.
"Ocha" means "Tea", "Ohana" means "Flower" in Japanese.
The cafe has Scandinavian and Japanese mixed taste, therefore, I added both essences to it. The circle part is designed to look like tea, flowers, or Montblanc.
