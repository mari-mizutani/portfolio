Tomoko Pilates NYC

Web Design, Frontend Development

Tomoko Pilates NYC is a Pilates business in NY.
I got an opportunity to renew the official website with WordPress.
To be honest, I didn't have much experience with WordPress, it was a bit challenging for me.
But, through this project, I gained lots of knowledge about it. For instance how to create blogs, how to customize the pages with plugins etc.
Especially creating a multilingual website (this time English and Japanese) was interesting because I had to consider not only languages but also each culture.
Also, it was truly a good opportunity to learn how I should manage from design to develop the websites.
